# Module 6: Verbal Ability

## 1. Grammar
**Q1. One of the boys / who always give / the correct answer / is Samuel.**
**Sol:** "One of the + Plural + who/which + Plural Verb". 'give' is correct. No error.

**Q2. The sceneries / of Kashmir / is very charming.**
**Sol:** 'Scenery' is uncountable (no plural). Error: 'sceneries' $\rightarrow$ 'scenery'.

**Q3. I have been / working on this project / since three years.**
**Sol:** 'Since' for point in time, 'For' for duration. Error: 'since' $\rightarrow$ 'for'.

**Q4. Correct spelling: Ommission, Omission, Ommision, Omision?**
**Sol:** Omission.

**Q5. Antonym of 'OBSCURE':**
**Sol:** Obscure = unclear. Antonym = Explicit (clear).

## 2. Para Jumbles
**Q1. R. Swami lived in village. Q. People came. P. He was busy. S. Continued 3 days.**
**Sol:** RQPS.

**Q2. D. Arrived midnight. B. First visit. C. Decided taxi. A. But didn't know how.**
**Sol:** DBCA.

**Q3. 1. Stole meat. Q. Cross bridge. P. Looked down. R. Thought another dog. S. Barked. 6. Lost meat.**
**Sol:** QPRS.
