# Module 6: Verbal Ability

## 1. Grammar
**Q1. One of the boys / who always give / the correct answer / is Samuel.**
**Sol:**
Rule: "One of the + Plural Noun + who/which + Plural Verb"
Antecedent is "boys" (Plural)
Verb "give" is Plural
Result = No error.

**Q2. The sceneries / of Kashmir / is very charming.**
**Sol:**
Rule: Uncountable nouns have no plural form.
Word 'Scenery' is uncountable.
Error: 'sceneries' $\rightarrow$ 'scenery'

**Q3. I have been / working on this project / since three years.**
**Sol:**
Rule: 'Since' = point in time, 'For' = duration.
"Three years" is a duration.
Error: 'since' $\rightarrow$ 'for'

**Q4. Correct spelling: Ommission, Omission, Ommision, Omision?**
**Sol:**
Root = Omit
Suffix = ion
Rule: Single 'm', double 's'.
Ans = Omission

**Q5. Antonym of 'OBSCURE':**
**Sol:**
Meaning of Obscure = Unclear / Hidden
Opposite meaning = Clear / Detailed
Ans = Explicit

## 2. Para Jumbles
**Q1. R. Swami lived in village. Q. People came. P. He was busy. S. Continued 3 days.**
**Sol:**
Start = R (Introduces character and setting)
Follows = Q (Action happening at setting)
Follows = P (Reaction to the action)
End = S (Duration of the entire event)
Ans = RQPS

**Q2. D. Arrived midnight. B. First visit. C. Decided taxi. A. But didn't know how.**
**Sol:**
Start = D (Initial event)
Follows = B (Context of the event)
Follows = C (Decision made due to context)
End = A (Hurdle to the decision)
Ans = DBCA

**Q3. 1. Stole meat. Q. Cross bridge. P. Looked down. R. Thought another dog. S. Barked. 6. Lost meat.**
**Sol:**
Start = 1 (Given)
Follows = Q (Journey begins)
Follows = P (Action on journey)
Follows = R (Thought caused by action)
Follows = S (Reaction caused by thought)
End = 6 (Given)
Ans = QPRS
