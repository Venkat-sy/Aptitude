# Module 5: Data Interpretation

## 1. Data Analysis (Based on Tables)

**Instruction:** *Study the following table and answer the questions based on it. The table shows the production of various types of vehicles (in thousands) over the given years.*

| Year | Type A | Type B | Type C | Type D | Type E | Total |
|------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|-------|
| 1998 | 27     | 34     | 23     | 9      | 3      | 96    |
| 1999 | 29     | 40     | 31     | 12     | 4      | 116   |
| 2000 | 25     | 42     | 35     | 16     | 5      | 123   |
| 2001 | 30     | 45     | 40     | 18     | 6      | 139   |
| 2002 | 34     | 51     | 45     | 21     | 8      | 159   |

1. In which year was the percentage of production of Type E vehicles to the total production of vehicles the maximum?

A) 1998

B) 1999

C) 2001

D) 2002

**Answer:** Option **D**

**Explanation:**

Percentage of Type E out of Total:

For 1998 = (3 / 96) x 100 = 3.125%

For 1999 = (4 / 116) x 100 = 3.44%

For 2000 = (5 / 123) x 100 = 4.06%

For 2001 = (6 / 139) x 100 = 4.31%

For 2002 = (8 / 159) x 100 = 5.03%

The maximum percentage is in the year 2002.

2. The percentage increase in the production of Type B vehicles from 1998 to 2002 is:

A) 45%

B) 50%

C) 55%

D) 60%

**Answer:** Option **B**

**Explanation:**

Production of Type B in 1998 = 34.

Production of Type B in 2002 = 51.

Increase = 51 - 34 = 17.

Percentage increase = (17 / 34) x 100 %

= (1 / 2) x 100 %

= 50%.

3. The production of Type C vehicles in 2001 is approximately what percent of the total production of vehicles in 1999?

A) 32%

B) 34.5%

C) 36%

D) 40%

**Answer:** Option **B**

**Explanation:**

Production of Type C in 2001 = 40.

Total production in 1999 = 116.

Required Percentage = (40 / 116) x 100 %

= (10 / 29) x 100 %

= 1000 / 29 %

= 34.48% (Approx 34.5%).

4. What is the ratio of the total production of Type A vehicles for the given years to the total production of Type C vehicles for the given years?

A) 4 : 5

B) 5 : 6

C) 6 : 7

D) 7 : 8

**Answer:** Option **B**

**Explanation:**

Total production of Type A = 27 + 29 + 25 + 30 + 34 = 145.

Total production of Type C = 23 + 31 + 35 + 40 + 45 = 174.

Required Ratio = 145 : 174

Divide both sides by 29.

Ratio = 5 : 6.

5. In which year was the total production of all types of vehicles exactly equal to the average total production over the given years?

A) 1999

B) 2000

C) 2001

D) None of the above

**Answer:** Option **D**

**Explanation:**

Sum of total production for all years = 96 + 116 + 123 + 139 + 159 = 633.

Average total production = 633 / 5 = 126.6.

Looking at the total column (96, 116, 123, 139, 159), none of them is exactly 126.6.

Therefore, the answer is None of the above.
