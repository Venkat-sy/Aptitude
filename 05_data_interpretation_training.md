# Training Module 5: Data Interpretation

## 1. Data Analysis (Based on Tables/Graphs)

**Instruction:** *Study the following table and answer the questions based on it. (Data indicates thousands of units)*

| Year | Type A | Type B | Type C | Type D | Type E | Total |
|------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|-------|
| 1998 | 27     | 34     | 23     | 9      | 3      | 96    |
| 1999 | 29     | 40     | 31     | 12     | 4      | 116   |
| 2000 | 25     | 42     | 35     | 16     | 5      | 123   |
| 2001 | 30     | 45     | 40     | 18     | 6      | 139   |
| 2002 | 34     | 51     | 45     | 21     | 8      | 159   |

**Q1. In which year was the percentage of production of Type E vehicles to the total production of vehicles the maximum?**
> **Solution:** Calculate (Type E / Total) for each year.
> 1998: 3/96 = ~3.1%
> 1999: 4/116 = ~3.4%
> 2000: 5/123 = ~4.06%
> 2001: 6/139 = ~4.3%
> 2002: 8/159 = ~5.03%
> Maximum is in 2002.

**Q2. The percentage increase in the production of Type B vehicles from 1998 to 2002 is:**
> **Solution:** Production of B in 1998 = 34.
> Production of B in 2002 = 51.
> Increase = 51 - 34 = 17.
> Percentage increase = (17 / 34) * 100 = 50%.

**Q3. The production of Type C vehicles in 2001 is approximately what percent of the total production of vehicles in 1999?**
> **Solution:** Type C in 2001 = 40.
> Total in 1999 = 116.
> Percentage = (40 / 116) * 100 = (10/29) * 100 = ~34.48%.

**Q4. What is the ratio of the total production of Type A vehicles for the given years to the total production of Type C vehicles for the given years?**
> **Solution:** Total Type A = 27 + 29 + 25 + 30 + 34 = 145.
> Total Type C = 23 + 31 + 35 + 40 + 45 = 174.
> Ratio = 145 : 174. Both divisible by 29. (145/29) : (174/29) = 5 : 6.

**Q5. In which year was the total production of all types of vehicles exactly equal to the average total production over the given years?**
> **Solution:** Total of totals = 96 + 116 + 123 + 139 + 159 = 633.
> Average total = 633 / 5 = 126.6.
> None of the years has a total exactly equal to 126.6. The closest is 2000 (123).
