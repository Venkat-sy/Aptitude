## 1. Percentages
**Q1. Two students appeared at an examination. One secured 9 marks more than the other and his marks was 56% of the sum of their marks. Marks are:**
*A. 39,30* | *B. 41,32* | *C. 42,33* | *D. 43,34*
**Sol:**
$x+9 = 0.56(2x+9)$
$25(x+9) = 14(2x+9)$
$3x = 99$
$x = 33$
Marks: 33, 42
*(Ans: C)*
**Trick:**
Sum in Option C = 75
$56\%$ of $75 = 42$ (Matches)

**Q2. If 20% of a = b, then b% of 20 is:**
*A. 4% of a* | *B. 5% of a* | *C. 20% of a*
**Sol:**
$b = 0.2a$
Req: $b\%$ of $20$
$= (0.2a / 100) \times 20$
$= 0.04a$
$= 4\%$ of $a$
*(Ans: A)*

**Q3. Population increased from 1,75,000 to 2,62,500 in a decade. Average percent increase per year is:**
*A. 4.37%* | *B. 5%* | *C. 6%*
**Sol:**
Increase = $262500 - 175000 = 87500$
Total $\%$ inc = $(87500 / 175000) \times 100 = 50\%$
Per year avg = $50\% / 10 = 5\%$
*(Ans: B)*

**Q4. A fruit seller sells 40% apples and still has 420. Originally, he had:**
*A. 588* | *B. 600* | *C. 672* | *D. 700*
**Sol:**
Left = $100\% - 40\% = 60\%$
$0.6x = 420$
$x = 420 / 0.6$
$x = 700$
*(Ans: D)*

**Q5. Election between two: one got 55% valid votes. 20% votes invalid. Total votes = 7500. Valid votes for other candidate:**
*A. 2700* | *B. 2900* | *C. 3000*
**Sol:**
Valid votes = $80\%$ of $7500 = 6000$
Other got = $100\% - 55\% = 45\%$ of Valid
$= 0.45 \times 6000$
$= 2700$
*(Ans: A)*

## 2. Profit & Loss
**Q1. CP of 20 articles = SP of x articles. Profit = 25%. x = ?**
*A. 15* | *B. 16* | *C. 18*
**Sol:**
Let $CP\ of\ 1 = 1$
$SP(x) = CP(20) = 20$
$Profit\% = \left(\frac{SP - CP}{CP}\right) \times 100 = 25$
$\left(\frac{20 - x}{x}\right) = \frac{1}{4}$
$80 - 4x = x$
$5x = 80$
$x = 16$
*(Ans: B)*

**Q2. Vendor bought toffees at 6 for Re 1. How many for Re 1 must he sell to gain 20%?**
*A. 3* | *B. 4* | *C. 5*
**Sol:**
$CP = 1/6$
Required $SP = (1/6) \times 1.20$
$SP = 1/5$
Sells 5 for Re 1
*(Ans: C)*

**Q3. Buys cycle for Rs. 1400, sells at 15% loss. SP = ?**
*A. 1090* | *B. 1160* | *C. 1190*
**Sol:**
$Loss = 15\%$
$SP = 85\%$ of $CP$
$SP = 0.85 \times 1400$
$SP = 1190$
*(Ans: C)*

**Q4. Bought at 6 for Rs. 5, sold at 5 for Rs. 6. Gain % = ?**
*A. 30%* | *B. 33.3%* | *C. 44%*
**Sol:**
Total CP = $5 \times 5 = 25$
Total SP = $6 \times 6 = 36$
Gain = $36 - 25 = 11$
$Gain\% = (11 / 25) \times 100 = 44\%$
*(Ans: C)*

**Q5. Selling at 800 -> 25% profit. SP for 25% loss = ?**
*A. 720* | *B. 640* | *C. 480*
**Sol:**
$1.25 \times CP = 800$
$CP = 640$
Req $SP = 0.75 \times 640$
$SP = 480$
*(Ans: C)*

## 4. SI & CI
**Q1. SI amounts to Rs 815 in 3 yrs, 854 in 4 yrs. Sum = ?**
*A. 650* | *B. 690* | *C. 698*
**Sol:**
$SI(1yr) = 854 - 815 = 39$
$SI(3yr) = 39 \times 3 = 117$
$Sum = 815 - 117 = 698$
*(Ans: C)*

**Q2. Rate % per annum for 1200 to become 1348.32 in 2 yrs CI?**
*A. 6%* | *B. 6.5%* | *C. 7%*
**Sol:**
$1200 \times \left(1 + \frac{R}{100}\right)^2 = 1348.32$
$\left(1 + \frac{R}{100}\right)^2 = 1.1236$
$\left(1 + \frac{R}{100}\right)^2 = 1.06^2$
$1 + \frac{R}{100} = 1.06$
$R = 6$
*(Ans: A)*

**Q3. SI for 2.5 yrs @ 12% is Rs 40 less than SI for 3.5 yrs @ 10%. Sum = ?**
*A. 600* | *B. 666* | *C. 800*
**Sol:**
$SI_1 = P \times 10\% \times 3.5 = 0.35P$
$SI_2 = P \times 12\% \times 2.5 = 0.30P$
$0.35P - 0.30P = 40$
$0.05P = 40$
$P = 800$
*(Ans: C)*

**Q4. 60% increase in 6 yrs at SI. CI of 12000 after 3 yrs at same rate?**
*A. 2160* | *B. 3120* | *C. 3972*
**Sol:**
$R = \frac{60}{6} = 10\%$
$Amount = 12000 \times (1.1)^3$
$Amount = 15972$
$CI = 15972 - 12000 = 3972$
*(Ans: C)*

**Q5. Diff between CI and SI on sum for 2 yrs @ 4% is Re 1. Sum = ?**
*A. 625* | *B. 630* | *C. 640*
**Sol:**
$D = P \times \left(\frac{R}{100}\right)^2$
$1 = P \times \left(\frac{4}{100}\right)^2$
$1 = P \times \left(\frac{1}{625}\right)$
$P = 625$
*(Ans: A)*

## 5. Averages & Ages
**Q1. Avg of 20 numbers is 0. At most how many > 0?**
*A. 0* | *B. 1* | *C. 19*
**Sol:**
Sum = 0
19 can be huge positive numbers
1 can be a massive negative number to balance
Max > 0 = 19
*(Ans: C)*

**Q2. Captain=26, Keeper=29. Team avg=x. Exclude them, avg=x-1. Team avg = ?**
*A. 23* | *B. 24* | *C. 25*
**Sol:**
Total age = $11x$
Remaining 9 players sum = $11x - (26+29) = 11x - 55$
Avg of 9 = $\frac{11x - 55}{9} = x - 1$
$11x - 55 = 9x - 9$
$2x = 46$
$x = 23$
*(Ans: A)*

**Q3. F+S=60. 6 yrs ago, F-6 = 5(S-6). S in 6 yrs = ?**
*A. 12* | *B. 14* | *C. 20*
**Sol:**
$F = 60 - S$
$60 - S - 6 = 5S - 30$
$54 - S = 5S - 30$
$6S = 84$
$S = 14$
$S_{in 6 yrs} = 14 + 6 = 20$
*(Ans: C)*

**Q4. M = S+24. In 2 yrs, M+2 = 2(S+2). S = ?**
*A. 14* | *B. 22* | *C. 24*
**Sol:**
Substitute M:
$(S+24) + 2 = 2(S+2)$
$S + 26 = 2S + 4$
$S = 22$
*(Ans: B)*

**Q5. Avg H,W,C 3 yrs ago=27. Avg W,C 5 yrs ago=20. Present H = ?**
*A. 35* | *B. 40* | *C. 50*
**Sol:**
Present sum(H,W,C) = $(27 \times 3) + (3 \times 3) = 90$
Present sum(W,C) = $(20 \times 2) + (5 \times 2) = 50$
$H = 90 - 50 = 40$
*(Ans: B)*
